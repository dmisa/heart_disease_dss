Our Dataset.

The purpose of our site is to predict if a person has heart disease. To do so we used a dataset which contains 13 attributes (age,sex,resting blood pressure,chest, serum cholestoral,fasting blood sugar,resting electrocardiographic results, maximum heart rate achieved,exercise induced angina,oldpeak,slope,number of major vessels,thal). The data was collected from 269 samples. We then processed our data on Weka , trying different filters and classifiers to get the best possible results.

How we used weka for classification

Preprocess Attribute Selection

We used attribute selection to increase the precision of the algorithm for classification. Moreover, precision of J48 algorithm became 0.811. It was 0.766. Tp rate was 0.767 and became 0.811. Attributes that derived from attribute selection are: 1)chest, 2)resting electrocardiographic results, 3)max heart rate, 4)exercise induces angina, 5)oldpeak, 6)number of major vessels, 7)thal. So user must fill in only these 7 attributes in the form. Click image to download!

Classify J-48 algorithm

We used J-48 algorithm for classification because it was more precise than all tested algorithms (tp rate, precision). We tested 1)Naive Bayes, IBK, J48, PARTW. Click image to download!

Cross-validation 10 folds

We used cross-validation with 10 folds because it is more valid than training and testing. Training and testing had higher percentage of precision but 10 fold cross-validation had high precision and higher percentage of tp-rate than training+testing.
